Exceptions

A lot of Charlianic words have exceptions. Here are a list of them:

Past Participles
Nouns

Here is a list of exceptional past participles:
arestir, to stop : paus
decir, to repeat : rejpetej
ir, to have: eod
estir, to be: seom
etir, to go: venit
iskir, to search: ix



Here is a list of exceptional nouns:
arbo, tree: arbom, tree
fuoro, anger: fuorom, anger
futuro, future: futurom, future
forto, might/strength: fortom, might/strength
du,right: duam(singular) / duas(plural) , (human) rights
cenerasi, generation: cenerasim, generation
istori, history: istoriam, history
cimat, button: cimiat, button
